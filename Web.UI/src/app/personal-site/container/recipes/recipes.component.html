Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

Tikka Paste

  • 1tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 7 cloves garlic
  • 1 2inch piece ginger
  • 1-2 red chillis
  • 1/4cup (60ml) water
  • 2tbsp (30ml) olive oil

Chicken Marinade

  • 1 cup (225g) yogurt
  • 1kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp (26g) of tikka paste
  • 2 teaspoons (7g) kosher salt

The Sauce

  • 2 cans whole peeled tomatoes, blended and strained
  • 4 tbsp (56g) unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion
  • 300ml cup double cream
  • 1 tbsp paprika
